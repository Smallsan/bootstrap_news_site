Sheena Halili and family dance to BINI’s ‘Pantropiko’ (goodvibes)

On Instagram, the celebrity mom shared an adorable video of their family dancing to the beat of BINI’s hit “Pantropiko.”

In the video, their daughter Martina can be seen in the middle doing her own version of dance steps, while her parents happily followed her dance moves.

“Halili-Manzanero PANTROPIKO dance moves!” Sheena wrote in the caption.

Their dance version has quickly earned approval from netizens, with some of them calling Martina the “dance leader.” Even Camille Prats can’t help but gush over the little one’s cute moves and even asked her to dance with her.
