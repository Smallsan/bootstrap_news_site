Marian Rivera brings back Marimar in new TikTok dance video

On TikTok, the Kapuso Primetime Queen shared a video of herself doing her makeup, before the reel transitioned into her dancing to the series intro.

"Bring you back to 2007- Asoka style," she captioned.

She teased the video a day before, saying, "Challenge accepted!"

Marian and her husband Dingdong Dantes headlined the iconic "Marimar" series aired on GMANetwork in 2007. In 2015, a reboot was aired starring Megan Young and Tom Rodriguez.
